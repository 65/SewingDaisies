About Me

My name is Heidi...and like you I am trying to find that elusive creative gene.

When I was young I dreamt of being a children's presenter. You know the ones that used to make rockets from squeegee bottles and sticky tape, but somewhere along the way that dream was put to one side.

That is until now.

What is Sewing Daisies?

Sewing Daisies is my attempt at living my childhood dream job.

Instead of building rockets however, I try to create lovely things to share with you.

Some projects work, others not so much.

As I am learning as I go along, I welcome your input and tips!

What will you find on Sewing Daisies?

A mix of crochet, paper art, sewing and printing projects that I have completed with a good smattering of inspiration from other bloggers and flickr friends whom I admire.

There are also how-to's available for some projects.

I hope you enjoy Sewing Daisies and I look forward to meeting you.

P.S. I live 'down-under' (Sydney, Australia).
